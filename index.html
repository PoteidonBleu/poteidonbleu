About Us

Learn More About Us

Poteidon Bleu is a modern and innovative take on the classic chicken cordon bleu, a beloved dish traditionally served during special occasions and celebrations. However, we wanted to make this delicious experience more accessible, so we’ve transformed the dish with a potato-based alternative that can be enjoyed anytime, anywhere—not just during special moments. Our Potato Cordon Bleu is available in four distinctive and flavorful variations, each carefully crafted to offer a unique twist on the original.


To complement the flavors, we serve it with bechamel sauce—the mother of all sauces—rich, smooth, and creamy, bringing a comforting texture that enhances every bite. At Poteidon Bleu, we are passionate about transforming the ordinary into the extraordinary, offering a fresh perspective on a timeless favorite. Our goal is to bring you a delightful, satisfying, and versatile dining experience that makes every meal feel like a celebration, no matter the occasion.

Our Mission

We aim to make the product affordable and healthy for a better experience so that people may enjoy it without any kind of limitations. We want to satisfy client needs and enhance their mental and physical well-being, strive to provide unique, enticing, and nourishing kinds of food, and an alternative healthy version of cordon bleu.

Our Vision

Envision a future where our Potato Cordon Bleu becomes the primary go-to type of food for every individual across the country and is recognized as the most excellent potato product in the nation, not just in one city. As we dedicate ourselves to producing quality and affordable products, we plan to serve consumers with our utmost effort during operation to surpass their expectations and commit to performance and innovation.
